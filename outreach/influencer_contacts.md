### About Party Games:

"Party Games" is a groundbreaking mobile party word game platform that combines viral 
word chain mechanics with fast-paced trivia challenges. Built specifically for iOS devices, 
the app offers immediate playability without complex download processes - perfect for 
college parties, social gatherings, and event planning.

---

### Key Features:

✓ **Viral Word Chain Mechanics**: Players build words from each other's final letters, creating 
   chain reactions that spread across social media platforms
   
✓ **Social Guessing Mode**: Collaborative game mode supporting 4-8 players simultaneously
   
✓ **Trivia Blitz Challenge**: Fast-paced knowledge tests with timed rounds and achievement tracking
   
✓ **iOS-Optimized PWA**: Works instantly on iPhone/iPad without traditional app store download
   
✓ **Offline Play**: Service worker caching enables gameplay even without internet connection
