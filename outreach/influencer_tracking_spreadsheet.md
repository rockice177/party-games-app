## Tracking Methods

### Response Rate Targets:
- **TikTok/Reels Influencers**: 20-30% response rate to demo email
- **YouTube Creators**: 10-15% response rate  
- **Press Contacts**: 15-20% pickup rate for press release
- **Community Subreddits**: 60-80% engagement with posts

### Conversion Funnel:
```
Email Sent → Response Received (30%) 
→ Demo Link Clicked (70% of responders)  
→ App Downloaded/Played (50% of demo viewers)  
→ Social Media Post Created (25% of downloaded users)
```

### Budget ROI Calculator:
- Cost per influencer contact: $1.50 avg ($3K budget / 2,000 emails)
- Average conversion rate to post: 5% of contacted influencers
- Cost per successful integration: $60 (avg)
- Expected posts: 100 influencers × 5% = 5 posts first week
- Each post avg views: 5K-20K → 250K-1M total impressions
